PA Activities

Here are some of the things we do as PA:

  • Plan and run the Holiday Gift Shop in December
  • Organize the annual Picture Day
  • Fund the fifth grade yearbook, T-shirts and graduation party
  • Survey the school community and work with the administration to enhance our children's experience through afterschool programs and enrichment activities
  • Apply for grants and reach out to local government officials to raise money for large projects such as the schoolyard renovations

How can you contribute to the PA?

Here are some ways you can help make a difference

  • Attend our monthly meetings: Schedule & Minutes
  • Sign up to our mailing list
  • Volunteer some of your time to help with the Holiday Shop or other events
  • Email the PA board any ideas you have for improving our school and community to 22k134pa@schools.nyc.gov
  • Donate to help fund PA endeavors by using our Zelle and Venmo accounts
